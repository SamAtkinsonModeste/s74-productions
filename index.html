S74 PRODUCTIONS Ltd

S74 Productions Ltd is based in Bromley, Kent. We specialize in all aspects of entertainment from Musical Theatre Productions to Corporate film and video. Our team consists of a passionate group of Creatives who are ready to carry out your production idea from the first “brainstorming” stage to the final product.
Our aim is to nurture and develop your ideas and transform these into a phenomenal visual product! We work with writers, designers, stylists, directors, choreographers, actors, dancers, make-up and hair technicians, to name a few. Our job is to put together the best creative team working for your project with your individual budget and vision. We have all the tools in place to carry your project from start to finish however big or small.

Up and Coming Projects

S74 Productions have numerous projects they are working on in various fields. Currently they are focusing on their live Musical Theatre production, SMILE. This is an original musical written, choreographed, directed and produced by their in house creative team. Opening its doors on Sunday 18th October 2015 at the The Churchill Theatre in Bromley, SMILE promises to deliver exactly what is says on the tin. With a potential UK tour, S74 Productions are very excited for the upcoming year. Alongside their Musical Theatre projects the team are also working on children’s TV series, promotional packages for businesses and up and coming artists, plus personal artiste management. Two of their young talents are about to embark on their first UK tour.

PROJECTS

To date S74 Productions have predominantly worked within children’s TV and theatre. Last year they produced a Musical Showcase based on a well-known classic 80’s film using original music and script created by their in house team of songwriters, choreographers & directors. This very exciting project is currently in development. They have also worked closely and personally with up and coming artists to create their whole bespoke package. From songs to pop videos to complete artist management S74 really observe and nurture the individual artists until their visions and goals come to light and become reality.
